About ABC School

Our History

Founded in 2000 by visionary educators Mr. and Mrs. Gupta, ABC School began as a small institution with just 50 students in a modest building. Recognizing the need for quality education in Nagpur, they started with a mission to provide accessible and high-quality learning opportunities.

Over the years, we have grown exponentially. In 2010, we expanded our campus to include modern facilities. By 2015, our student enrollment reached 1000, and we introduced advanced programs in STEM education. Today, ABC School stands as a leading educational establishment, serving over 1500 students from diverse backgrounds and communities.

Our Vision

To nurture young minds and empower them to become responsible, innovative, and successful individuals who contribute positively to society. We envision a world where our students lead with integrity, creativity, and compassion.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering comprehensive education that combines academic excellence with holistic development, including physical, emotional, and social growth. Our mission is to create lifelong learners who are equipped to face the challenges of the 21st century.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education and conduct.
  • Integrity: Honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all interactions.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking approaches.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming diversity and ensuring equal opportunities for all.
  • Community: Building strong partnerships with parents and the local community.
