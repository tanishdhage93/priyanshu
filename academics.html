Academic Programs

At ABC School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet the needs of students at every stage of their educational journey. Our programs are aligned with the Maharashtra State Board syllabus while incorporating innovative teaching methods and technology.

Primary Education (Classes 1–5)

Focus on foundational skills in reading, writing, mathematics, and basic sciences. Emphasis on creative learning and play-based education. Our primary program includes experiential learning activities that make education fun and engaging.

Syllabus Highlights:

  • English Language and Literature (reading, writing, comprehension)
  • Mathematics (numbers, basic operations, geometry)
  • Environmental Science (nature, animals, plants, weather)
  • Social Studies (community, family, basic geography)
  • Art and Craft (drawing, painting, handicrafts)
  • Physical Education (games, yoga, basic sports)
  • Moral Science (values, ethics, social skills)

Assessment is continuous through projects, activities, and regular tests to ensure holistic development.

Secondary Education (Classes 6–10)

Building on primary education, students explore advanced concepts in various subjects, preparing for board examinations. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and practical application of knowledge.

Syllabus Highlights:

  • English (literature, grammar, writing skills)
  • Hindi/Sanskrit (language and literature)
  • Mathematics (algebra, geometry, statistics)
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology - theory and practical)
  • Social Science (History, Geography, Civics, Economics)
  • Computer Science (programming basics, digital literacy)
  • Physical Education (sports, health education)
  • Art Education (visual arts, music)

Students participate in science fairs, math Olympiads, and cultural competitions to apply their learning.

Higher Secondary (Classes 11–12)

Specialized streams to prepare students for higher education and career paths. Our senior program includes career counseling and college preparation workshops.

Streams Offered:

  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology/Computer Science, English
  • Commerce: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Mathematics/Informatics Practices, English
  • Arts: History, Geography, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, English

Optional subjects allow students to pursue their interests while maintaining a strong foundation in core subjects.

Teaching Methodology

We employ a blend of traditional and modern teaching methods:

  • Interactive classroom sessions with multimedia aids
  • Project-based learning and group activities
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Individualized attention for students with different learning needs
  • Integration of technology in daily lessons
