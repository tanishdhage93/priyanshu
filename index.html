School Overview

Established in 2000, ABC School has been a cornerstone of education in Nagpur, offering comprehensive programs from primary to higher secondary levels. Our school is affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board and follows a curriculum that balances academic rigor with extracurricular activities.

We pride ourselves on our diverse student body, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities that create an ideal learning environment.
